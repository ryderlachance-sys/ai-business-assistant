Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 9, 2025

AI Business Assistant (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and authentication details when you sign in or connect integrations.
  • Integration Data: Tokens and metadata required to connect third-party services (for example, Google Workspace, Slack, or CRM platforms). We only store the minimum necessary data to maintain connections.
  • Usage Data: Log files, device details, and analytics events that help us improve product performance and security.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and personalize the AI Business Assistant dashboard and automation tools.
  • Process integration requests and perform actions on your behalf when instructed (emails, calendar events, analytics).
  • Improve reliability, detect abuse, and develop new features.
  • Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and administrative messages.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who assist with hosting, analytics, error tracking, or customer support. They are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Integrations: Third-party services you explicitly connect (for example, Gmail or Slack). Data shared is limited to the scope authorized during OAuth consent.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Access tokens and integration metadata are removed when you disconnect an integration or delete your account.

5. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data. This includes encryption in transit, scoped access controls, and continuous monitoring. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access, correct, or delete personal data stored with us.
  • Revoke integration permissions directly in the dashboard or within the third-party provider.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications by using unsubscribe links or contacting support.

To exercise these rights, email support@ai-business-assistant.com.

7. International Transfers

Depending on your location, personal data may be processed in countries with different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses, are in place when transferring data.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years old. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a minor, we will take steps to delete it.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes become effective when posted on this page, and we will notify you of material updates.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or our data practices, contact us at privacy@ai-business-assistant.com.
